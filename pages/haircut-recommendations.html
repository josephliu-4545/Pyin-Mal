Understanding Face Shapes

Let’s Check

Oval Face

Balanced proportions with slightly wider cheekbones

  • Forehead slightly wider than jaw
  • Rounded jawline
  • Face length about 1.5 times width

Round Face

Similar width and length with soft angles

  • Widest at cheeks
  • Rounded jawline
  • Fuller cheeks

Square Face

Strong jawline and forehead with similar width

  • Angular jawline
  • Forehead and jaw similar width
  • Minimal curve at hairline

Heart Face

Wider forehead tapering to a narrow chin

  • Wide forehead and cheekbones
  • Narrow jawline
  • Pointed chin

Diamond Face

Narrow forehead and jaw with wide cheekbones

  • Widest at cheekbones
  • Narrow forehead and jaw
  • Pointed chin

Triangle Face

Wide jawline with a narrow forehead

  • Wide jawline
  • Narrow forehead
  • Strong chin
