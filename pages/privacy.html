Privacy policy

How Pyin Mal collects, uses, and protects your data.

Your trust matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information Pyin Mal collects when you use our styling, wardrobe, and FaceMesh features, how we use it to power your experience, and the choices you have about your data.

At a glance

  • • We collect only the data we need to provide styling recommendations and improve the product.
  • • Face and camera data are handled with extra care and used only with your permission.
  • • We do not sell your personal data. Limited sharing happens only with essential service providers.

1. Who we are and scope of this policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Pyin Mal (“Pyin Mal”, “we”, “our”, “us”) handles personal information when you use our website, styling tools, FaceMesh and model previews, or interact with us through features such as pricing, reviews, or contact forms.

This policy applies to the Pyin Mal website and related services that link to it. By using our site, you agree to the practices described here, in addition to any country‑specific rights that may apply to you.

2. Information we collect

The specific information we collect depends on how you use Pyin Mal. In general, we may collect:

  • Account information – such as your name, email address, password (stored in encrypted form), and basic profile details you choose to share.
  • Style and preference data – including your selected body type, preferred fits, colors, style inspirations, saved items, favorites, and feedback on recommendations.
  • Camera and FaceMesh data – if you enable camera access or upload a photo for haircut try‑on, face shape analysis, or model previews, we receive image frames and facial landmark data required to run those features.
  • Device and usage information – such as browser type, device identifiers, language settings, approximate location based on IP, pages viewed, clicks, and interaction logs used for analytics and product improvement.
  • Communication data – when you contact us (for example via the Contact page), we collect the information you provide, such as your name, email address, and message content.

We may combine information across these categories to better understand how you use Pyin Mal and to provide a more coherent styling experience.

3. How we use your information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Providing and personalizing styling features – to generate outfit suggestions, haircut recommendations, and model previews tailored to your body type, preferences, and past activity.
  • Operating your account – to create and maintain your user profile, manage authentication, and remember your saved favorites and plans.
  • Improving Pyin Mal – to analyze how users interact with pages, filters, and tools so that we can refine recommendations, UX flows, and performance.
  • Communicating with you – to respond to your questions, send important service updates, and notify you of changes to our policies or features.
  • Security and fraud prevention – to protect our services, our users, and our infrastructure from abuse, suspicious activity, or violations of our terms.
  • Legal and compliance purposes – to comply with applicable laws, respond to lawful requests, and enforce our terms and policies.

Where required by law, we rely on your consent (for example, for certain cookies or camera access) or on our legitimate interests in providing and improving a secure, reliable styling platform.

4. FaceScan, FaceMesh, and camera permissions

Some Pyin Mal features, such as Haircut Try‑On (FaceMesh) or AI Model Preview, may request access to your device’s camera or allow you to upload a photo. These features are entirely optional, and you can use many parts of Pyin Mal without them.

  • Your consent is required – we will only access your camera or photos after you grant permission at the browser or device level.
  • How face data is processed – face images and facial landmark data are used to detect face shape, align styles to your face, and generate previews. Where possible, this processing happens locally in your browser using technologies such as FaceMesh.
  • Limited retention – we do not use face data to identify you as a real‑world person, and we do not build a biometric identity profile. If images must be sent to our servers to generate certain previews, we retain them only for as long as needed to provide the feature or as required by law.
  • Turning permissions off – you can revoke camera permissions at any time in your browser or device settings. Doing so may disable certain try‑on features but will not affect your account in general.

We treat face and camera data as especially sensitive and apply additional security and access controls where it is processed or stored.

5. Cookies and tracking technologies

Pyin Mal may use cookies and similar technologies to make the site work properly, remember your preferences, and understand how the platform is used.

  • Essential cookies – required to provide core functionality, such as keeping you logged in, saving your selections, and securing your session.
  • Preference cookies – used to remember settings like language, theme, or region so that the experience feels consistent across visits.
  • Analytics cookies – help us understand which pages and features are performing well, so we can improve styling flows and product decisions.

Depending on your region, you may see a banner or settings panel allowing you to accept, manage, or reject certain categories of cookies (for example, non‑essential analytics).

You can also control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling some cookies may impact the functionality of Pyin Mal.

6. Data security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against accidental or unlawful destruction, loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or other forms of unlawful processing.

  • Encryption in transit for data sent between your browser and our services.
  • Access controls so only authorized team members and service providers can access necessary data.
  • Regular review of systems and logs to monitor for unusual or suspicious activity.

No online service can guarantee absolute security, but we work continuously to strengthen our protections and respond promptly to any potential issues.

7. How we share your information

We do not sell your personal data. We limit sharing to situations necessary to operate and improve Pyin Mal, comply with the law, or protect our rights and users.

  • Service providers – we may share information with trusted vendors that help us host the website, send emails, provide analytics, or process limited data for previews. These providers are bound by confidentiality obligations and may only use data on our instructions.
  • Legal and safety – we may disclose information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Pyin Mal, our users, or the public.
  • Business changes – if we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to appropriate safeguards and notifications.

Whenever we share data, we aim to minimize what is shared and to ensure an appropriate level of protection through contracts and technical measures.

8. Your rights and choices

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information. These can include:

  • Access – the right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction – the right to ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion – the right to request deletion of your data, for example by closing your account, subject to certain legal or operational retention requirements.
  • Restriction and objection – the right to limit or object to certain types of processing, such as direct marketing or some forms of analytics.
  • Data portability – in some cases, the right to receive your data in a structured, commonly used format and to transmit it to another service.

You can exercise many of these rights directly in your account settings (where available) or by contacting us using the details below. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling your request.

9. How to contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, your data, or how Pyin Mal handles privacy, we’re here to help.

The easiest way to reach us is through the Contact page on our site. Please include enough detail for us to understand your request, and we will do our best to respond within a reasonable time.

10. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as we add new features, adopt new technologies, or respond to legal and regulatory changes.

When we make material changes, we will update the “last updated” date at the bottom of this page and, where appropriate, provide additional notice (for example, via a banner or email). We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

This Privacy Policy is intended to provide a clear, user‑friendly summary of our practices and does not replace any rights you may have under applicable privacy laws. For specific legal advice, please consult a qualified professional.
